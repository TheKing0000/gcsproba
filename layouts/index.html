What is GCS Visibility Suite?

Global Cloud Services Visibility Suite brings together a wide variety of GCS services/tools that are mature and follow CI/CD practices, making them a strong competitor to any 3rd party solutions we might purchase, with the added benefit that each tool was built with SAP in mind. The services/tools are also designed to be integrated with each other, essentially resulting in a unified and cooperative tool set.
